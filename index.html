MinimaSTEPS

A minimal, offline step tracker for iPhone

What is MinimaSTEPS?

MinimaSTEPS is a lightweight iOS app for tracking your daily steps with a clean, distraction-free interface.

  • CoreMotion only (no HealthKit)
  • Fully offline: no servers, no accounts, no tracking
  • Today / Week / Month views
  • Swipe-driven navigation
  • One setting: configurable daily step goal
  • Local history archive stored on-device
  • Manual backup via Export / Import (JSON)
  • Foreground-only behavior (no background modes)
  • Native iOS experience, built with SwiftUI

Important

  • Open the app regularly
  • MinimaSTEPS syncs recent step data while the app is in use
  • Opening the app periodically ensures your recent activity is stored in the local history archive
  • The app is designed for iPhone only (portrait mode, dark-only)
